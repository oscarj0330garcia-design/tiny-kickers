Welcome Parents

At Tiny Kickers, we create a positive, structured, and safe environment where children build confidence, learn teamwork, and fall in love with soccer. Every session is age-appropriate, engaging, and led by caring coaches you can trust.

About the Owner/Coach

As the founder of Tiny Kickers, I bring a wealth of soccer experience to our program. I have played soccer throughout my life, competing with Houston Dynamo's youth teams from U9 to U19. My passion for the sport led to numerous collegiate scholarships, including offers from St. Edwards, Sul Ross, Wabash, and other universities. While significant injuries ultimately prevented me from pursuing collegiate and professional soccer opportunities, this experience has strengthened my commitment to helping young athletes develop their skills and passion for the game. Additionally, while playing as an older U19 player, I mentored and trained younger Houston Dynamo teams, gaining valuable coaching insights that I now apply to our sessions.

Currently, I serve as a geometry teacher, where my expertise in engaging and motivating students has honed my ability to connect with children. I excel at keeping kids involved and making them feel accomplished, skills that directly translate to creating a positive and effective coaching environment.

Age Groups

3–4 Years

Fun games, movement, coordination, and basic ball familiarity in a playful environment.

5–6 Years

Introduction to dribbling, passing, and teamwork through fun drills and small games.

7–8 Years

Skill development, small matches, and early game understanding.

9–10 Years

Advanced skills, positioning, teamwork, and structured play.

Middle School (11–13 Years)

Competitive training with focus on tactics, game strategy, fitness, and competitive matches.

High School (14–18 Years)

Elite-level training emphasizing advanced techniques, match preparation, and development for club and competitive play.
